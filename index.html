Our Love Story

We met for a simple coffee that became four unforgettable hours of talking, laughing, and sharing our first kiss. Everything felt natural, easy, and right — and since then, we’ve been rarely apart…

For the proposal, a red carpet lined with rose petals led the way. Six heart balloons held six rainbow-colored cards, each sharing a cherished memory. The final card awaited at the end, before a giant rose heart and glowing “Will you marry me?” sign.

Even as the ring slipped nervously from my hands, the glowing heart-shaped ring box sealed a memory we’ll treasure forever.

We ended the evening with a movie-night themed meal, dancing, before heading back to our room to find a rose-petal-covered bed, flowers, chocolates, savouring the night.
